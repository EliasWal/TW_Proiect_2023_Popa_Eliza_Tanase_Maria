Baby Manager

The site for parents and parents-to-be of babies, preschoolers and teenagers.

It holds all the information concerning your child all-together and makes it easy to access or modify it.

By creating an account, you will have a loving space to save every step of your child!

Look at what you could benefit of:

A "Calendar" feature to keep track of the every day activities of your child, like sleeping and eating, without sticky notes all over your house.

A "Memories" feature to create an online personal journal that preserves countless of beautiful stories of your child, as well as pictures.

A "Medical" feature to have a clear image of your child's medical history, as well as document any medication they might need.

A "Friends" feature to remember all of the amazing people your child has encountered and loves to spend time with.

A "Media" feature to save in one place all of the important multimedia resources concerning your child's evolution.
