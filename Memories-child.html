Jane's memories

18/02/2023

Playing at kindergarten

"We have been struggling for a few months to make Jane feel safe at kindergaten.

Every day as soon as she would step into the kindergaten, she would start crying.

...

Since we didn't have any way of keeping her at home, we decided to continue to take her to the kindergarten, where she would be safe and would have other children to be around, and hope for the best.

Today, it was the first time she was happy to go there and I managed to take a picture of her playing.

She is more and more brave every day and I'm so proud of her!"

13/06/2020

Always a foodie

"Some parents have a battle with their child every day trying to convince them to eat their meal.

Our Jane has never caused us problems like this.

...

From fruits and vegetables, to yogurt, eggs and cheese, she eats them all.

Although she is not a fan of pasta, but maybe when she grows older we will try to give her pasta again.

Today she had rice, and it was finger-liking good!"
